Quick Glossary

CoreLogic (source = 0)
Tax & deed-based transaction records. Realtors and sales are filtered with source = 0 in both the sales and realtors tables.
📋
Zebra (source = 1)
MLS-based transaction records. Realtors and sales are filtered with source = 1 in both the sales and realtors tables.
👥
Unique Realtors
Distinct realtors counted by duplicate_uid who appeared as buyer agent, co-buyer agent, listing agent, or co-listing agent on at least one sale in the state for the given source and date range.
💰
Transactions
Total sales records (grouped by duplicate_uid) for the state, source, and date window. Total columns are the arithmetic sum of CoreLogic + Zebra — agents or deals may appear in both sources.
